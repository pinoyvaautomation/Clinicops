# Previous Client Outreach Script

Use this when reaching out to previous clients, business contacts, or clinic owners you already know.

The goal is:

- reconnect
- show the product
- get feedback
- convert a few into early clinics
- ask for referrals or testimonials

Do not lead with an investor ask.

## 1. Short Reconnect Message

Hi [Name], I hope you’ve been well.

I wanted to reconnect because I’ve been building something for small clinics called `ClinicOps`.

It helps clinics handle:

- online booking
- intake and consent
- self-service appointment changes
- staff workflow in one system

I thought of you because of our previous work together, and I’d value your honest feedback.

If you’re open to it, I can send a short demo.

## 2. If They Reply Positively

Thanks, I appreciate it.

ClinicOps is designed for small clinics that want a simpler workflow than calls, chat threads, and manual admin.

If you want, I can walk you through it quickly and show:

- public booking
- intake
- appointment handling
- Premium tools like messaging and waitlist

## 3. If They Are a Good Potential Customer

You might actually be a good fit for the early rollout.

We’re currently opening a founder offer for early clinics:

- `Premium at $19/month`
- locked for `12 months`
- for the first `50 clinics`

You can also start with Free if you just want to test it first.

## 4. If They Like The Product

That’s good to hear.

If it ends up being useful for your clinic, I’d also love:

- your honest feedback
- a short testimonial later
- or an introduction to 1-2 clinic owners who might benefit

## 5. Referral Ask

If you know any clinic owners or managers who are still handling bookings and follow-up manually, I’d appreciate an introduction.

We’re looking for the first `10-20 clinics` to help shape the product early.

## 6. Soft Investor Transition

Only use this if they clearly ask about growth, support, or business plans.

Right now the main focus is getting the first clinics live and making sure the product solves real daily problems well.

If you’re ever interested in helping beyond product feedback or referrals, I’d be open to that conversation too, but I’m prioritizing customer traction first.

## 8. Follow-Up Message

Hi [Name], just following up in case this is relevant for you or someone in your network.

I’m currently looking for the first `10-20 clinics` to try ClinicOps and help shape the product early.

If you’d like, I can send:

- a short demo
- founder offer details

## 9. Best Practices

- Keep the first message short
- Ask for feedback before asking for referrals
- Do not push an investment conversation too early
- Let traction come first
- Use warm contacts to build trust and early case studies
