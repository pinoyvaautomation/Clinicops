# ClinicOps 30-Day Marketing Plan

Use this plan to get the first `10-20 clinics` onto ClinicOps before scaling infrastructure or ad spend.

## 1. Core Goal

- Get `10-20 clinic owners` to sign up
- Convert `5-10 clinics` into paid `Premium`
- Validate that the offer, onboarding, and sales messaging are working

## 2. Launch Offer

Use one simple founder offer:

- `Founding Clinic Offer`
- `Premium at $19/month for 12 months`
- `First 50 clinics only`

Recommended promo code:

- `LAUNCH19`

## 3. Best First Target

Do not target every clinic type at once. Start with one or two niches:

- Dental clinics
- Aesthetic / skin clinics
- Small outpatient clinics
- Wellness / therapy clinics

Best-fit clinic profile:

- `1-10 staff`
- Still handling bookings through phone, Messenger, SMS, spreadsheets, or paper
- Owner or manager is still involved in daily operations

## 4. Value Proposition

Lead with outcomes, not software features:

- Reduce front-desk back-and-forth
- Let patients book online
- Let patients reschedule without calling
- Keep intake, appointments, and follow-up in one place
- Give growing clinics a clear upgrade path with Premium tools

## 5. 30-Day Rollout

### Week 1: Setup

- Finalize one public landing page
- Publish the founder offer clearly
- Set up Facebook Page
- Set up clinic demo data for screenshots and short videos
- Prepare one short booking demo video
- Prepare one short dashboard / staff workflow video

### Week 2: Organic Posting

- Publish `3-4` Facebook posts
- Publish `1-2` short demo clips
- Share founder story and product rationale
- Begin posting in relevant clinic-owner groups where allowed

### Week 3: Direct Outreach

- Contact `100-150` clinic owners or managers
- Use Facebook DM, email, LinkedIn, and local business listings
- Focus on one clear message:
  - online booking
  - self-service changes
  - intake forms
  - better clinic follow-up

### Week 4: Paid Test

- Run a small Meta campaign
- Budget: `USD 5-10/day` for `7-10 days`
- Objective:
  - `Lead`
  - or `Messages`
- Use one offer only:
  - `Founding Clinic Offer: $19/month for 12 months`

## 6. Weekly KPI Targets

### Week 1

- Landing page live
- Facebook Page live
- Promo code ready
- 2 demo videos recorded

### Week 2

- `3-4` Facebook posts published
- `10+` inbound conversations or comments

### Week 3

- `100-150` outreach messages sent
- `20-30` replies or demo conversations

### Week 4

- `10-20` clinic signups
- `5-10` Premium activations

## 7. Suggested Funnel

1. Facebook post, ad, or outreach message
2. Landing page or direct DM reply
3. Demo call or guided signup
4. Founder offer presented
5. Clinic starts Free or activates Premium
6. Follow up after `3-7 days`

## 8. Infrastructure Rule

Do not over-upgrade infrastructure before demand is real.

Stay lean until you see:

- `10+` paying clinics
- regular daily bookings
- multiple clinic staff active at once
- real messaging and notification volume

Then upgrade services based on usage.

## 9. Messaging Rules

Use simple language:

- Do not say `all-in-one digital transformation platform`
- Do say `online booking, intake, and follow-up in one system`

Use urgency carefully:

- `First 50 clinics only`
- `Price locked for 12 months`

## 10. Success Signal

This plan is working if:

- clinic owners understand the offer immediately
- demos convert into signups
- at least a few clinics activate Premium without heavy persuasion

If signups happen but Premium does not convert, improve:

- pricing explanation
- Premium feature explanation
- upgrade messaging inside the app
