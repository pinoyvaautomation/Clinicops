## Problem Section

Small clinics often lose time managing appointments through calls, chat messages, spreadsheets, and disconnected admin work.

ClinicOps brings the core workflow into one place so the team can:

- accept online bookings
- collect intake details
- let patients reschedule without calling
- keep clinic operations visible to staff and owners

## Features Section

### Online booking

Share your booking page on your website and let patients book online.

### Digital intake and consent

Collect intake details before the visit instead of managing them manually.

### Self-service appointment changes

Let patients cancel or reschedule using secure links.

### Staff workflow

Give your clinic one dashboard for appointments, patients, staff, and services.

### Premium tools

Upgrade to Premium for secure messaging, waitlist recovery, reminders, notifications, and branding tools.
