# ClinicOps FAQ For Clinic Owners

Use this in sales conversations, landing pages, or follow-up email.

## 1. What is ClinicOps?

ClinicOps is a clinic booking and operations system built for small clinics that want to manage online booking, intake, appointment changes, and staff workflow in one place.

## 2. Is ClinicOps for hospitals?

No. ClinicOps is best for `small and growing clinics`, not large hospital-style operations.

## 3. What does the Free plan include?

Free is designed for clinics getting started online.

It includes:

- online booking
- intake and consent
- self-service cancel and reschedule
- limited staff, services, and appointments

## 4. What does Premium include?

Premium is for clinics that need stronger daily operations.

It includes everything in Free, plus:

- secure messaging
- waitlist
- reminders
- notifications
- branding tools

## 5. Do patients need to register before booking?

No. Patients can book through the clinic booking page.

Some secure actions, such as messaging or appointment management, are handled through verified account or secure booking links.

## 6. Can patients reschedule without calling the clinic?

Yes. ClinicOps includes secure self-service cancel and reschedule flows.

## 7. Can we embed booking on our website?

Yes. Clinics can use the public booking link or the embeddable booking option.

## 8. Is messaging included for all plans?

No. Messaging is a `Premium` feature.

## 9. Is waitlist included for all plans?

No. Waitlist is a `Premium` feature.

## 10. Does ClinicOps work for small teams?

Yes. ClinicOps is especially designed for clinics with small teams that need structure without a heavy enterprise system.

## 11. What if our clinic grows?

That is the intended path:

- start with Free
- move to Premium when the clinic needs more operational efficiency

## 12. Can staff have different levels of access?

Yes. ClinicOps supports role-based access for:

- Admin
- Front Desk
- Doctor
- Nurse

## 13. Is there a founder offer?

## 14. Do we need a custom website to use it?

No. Clinics can use the public booking page directly even without a custom website.

## 15. What is the best clinic type for ClinicOps right now?

ClinicOps is a strong fit for:

- dental clinics
- aesthetic clinics
- outpatient clinics
- wellness or therapy clinics

## 16. How do we get started?

Start with:

- a Free account
